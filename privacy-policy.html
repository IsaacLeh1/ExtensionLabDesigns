Threat Detector Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 15, 2023

Version: 1.0

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Threat Detector extension collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your data while providing basic threat detection capabilities.

Information We Collect

Data Type Purpose Retention Period
URLs of websites you visit To scan for potential security issues 30 days
Security scan results To track detection history 30 days
Known threat indicators To identify potential issues Until browser session ends

All collected data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Scanning websites for potential security issues
  • Providing you with alerts about detected threats
  • Maintaining a history of your recent scans

Data Sharing

This extension does not share any of your data with third parties. All processing happens locally on your device.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Clear all stored data at any time via the extension options
  • Disable the extension temporarily or permanently
  • Uninstall the extension, which will remove all associated data

Security Capabilities and Limitations

Important: This extension provides basic threat detection for informational purposes only. It is not a replacement for comprehensive security software and cannot guarantee complete protection against all threats.

Our capabilities include:

  • Basic detection of known malicious domains
  • Identification of suspicious page elements
  • Monitoring for large data transmissions

Our limitations include:

  • Cannot detect all types of malware or security threats
  • Limited to threats that match our detection patterns
  • Does not prevent malicious actions, only detects and reports them

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will provide a notification when you next use the extension.
